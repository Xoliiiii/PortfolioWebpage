WEATHER APP

Melody Maker is a web application designed to spark creativity and exploration in music creation. Users can experiment with a variety of instruments and loops, drag-and-drop to build melodies, and even collaborate with others in real-time. It's a fun and accessible platform for anyone to create music, regardless of their musical background.
