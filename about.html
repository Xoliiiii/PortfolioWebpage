But hey, that's part of the journey, right?

Why frontend develpment?

I was drawn to front-end development because it feels like a complex art class to me. Just like a painter, I get to use color theory, composition, and even a bit of user psychology to create engaging user interfaces. But instead of a canvas, my code becomes the brush that brings the design to life. The process of transforming a static design into a fully functional and interactive experience is incredibly rewarding. It allows me to combine my passion for design with my technical skills, making front-end development the perfect blend of creativity and functionality for me.
