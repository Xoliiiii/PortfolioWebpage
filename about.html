ABOUT ME










Who is Xolisa?

Xolisa is a detail-oriented work in progress! By day, I am constantly at work trying to find my feet in tech. But I'm also someone who's constantly striving to learn and grow. My strengths definitely lie in project management , adaptability and curiousity – these allow me to work independently and in grows as well as constantly find ways to the get the job done in a timely manner. However, I'm still working on discipline , procrastination and inquisitiveness – which sometimes leads to want to take on too many projects at once which then kinda makes me run around like a chicken with my head off. But hey, that's part of the journey, right?

Why frontend develpment?

I was drawn to front-end development because it feels like a complex art class to me. Just like a painter, I get to use color theory, composition, and even a bit of user psychology to create engaging user interfaces. But instead of a canvas, my code becomes the brush that brings the design to life. The process of transforming a static design into a fully functional and interactive experience is incredibly rewarding. It allows me to combine my passion for design with my technical skills, making front-end development the perfect blend of creativity and functionality for me.

Biggest chanllenge you found with coding/learning to code?

As a naturally curious learner, I sometimes find myself juggling multiple coding projects at once. While this keeps things exciting and allows me to explore different areas of front-end development, it can also lead to a hefty workload. To tackle this challenge, I've developed better project management skills, prioritizing tasks and setting realistic deadlines. It's a constant learning process, but it helps me channel my curiosity into focused progress.

What do you like to do in your free time?

In my free time, I'm all about unwinding and letting my imagination wander. There's nothing quite like soaking up the sun with a good book and some music – it's the perfect escape from the daily grind. Recently, I've discovered a new guilty pleasure: playing The Sims! It allows me to tap into my creative side by designing dream homes and crafting unique stories for my virtual characters. It's a fun way to unwind and unleash my creativity in a different way.
